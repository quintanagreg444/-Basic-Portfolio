About Me

Starting from back when I was a young boy and my Dad told me, "Son nothing is impossible." I rember a few years later I challenged him on that. I took an interest early in life in things in the sky, stars, clouds, planes, birds, kites. You get the point. I told my Dad, "One day we (humans) will land a ship on the sun." My Dad laughed and said, "Pffft, there is NO way that will ever happen, Son!" I quickly retorted, "But, Dad you said NOTHING is impossible!

Today that is my vision in becoming a Full stack developer, as Dad said, "Nothing is impossible!"
